* I am currently a final-year PhD Student at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Northwestern Polytechnical
University (NPU), China, under the supervision of ([Prof. Yong Xia](http://jszy.nwpu.edu.cn/yongxia.html)). I am also a visiting student at the School of Computer Science, University of Adelaide (UoA), Australia, under the supervision of ([Prof. Chunhua Shen](https://cshen.github.io/)). 


* My focus is mainly on artificial intelligence in medical image analysis applications, aiming to develop smart solutions to assist medical professionals in lesion and anatomical structure segmentation, and disease diagnosis and therapy. 
Recently, I have been investigating semi-/weakly-/self-supervised deep learning to improve medical image analysis with limited annotations.
